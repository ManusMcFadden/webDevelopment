Our history:

Nestled in the heart of Leeds, Luigi's Pizzeria stands as a testament to the timeless tradition of Neapolitan pizza. Our story began in the early 2000s when Luigi Romano, a passionate pizzaiolo from Naples, decided to bring the authentic taste of his homeland to the bustling streets of Leeds.

Luigi's journey was fueled by the desire to share the joy of simple, yet exquisite, Italian cuisine. He envisioned a place where the warmth of Italian hospitality met the vibrancy of Leeds' culinary scene. With a stone oven imported from the outskirts of Naples and a recipe passed down through generations, Luigi's Pizzeria opened its doors, promising an experience that would transport diners straight to the sun-drenched terraces of Italy.

As the aroma of freshly baked pizza dough began to waft through the neighborhood, Luigi's Pizzeria quickly became a beloved staple. The restaurant's commitment to quality is unwavering, with each pizza crafted from the finest, locally-sourced ingredients and adorned with the vibrant flavors of San Marzano tomatoes, creamy buffalo mozzarella, and fresh basil leaves.

Over the years, Luigi's Pizzeria has not only served delectable dishes but also cultivated a community. It's a place where families gather, friends reunite, and memories are made over slices of pizza that echo the rich history of Naples. Our walls are adorned with black and white photographs of old Naples, and the melodies of Italian classics softly serenade our guests as they indulge in the culinary legacy Luigi bestowed upon Leeds.

Today, Luigi's Pizzeria stands as a slice of history, a corner of Naples in the heart of Leeds, where the legacy of Luigi Romano continues to thrive. We invite you to be part of our story, to savor the flavors that have charmed palates for decades, and to create your own moments in our little piece of Italy.

Benvenuti a Luigi's Pizzeria - where history, flavor, and community meet.
