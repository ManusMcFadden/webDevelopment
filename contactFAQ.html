FAQs:

  1. What are your opening hours? We're open from 11:30 AM to 11:00 PM, Monday through Saturday, and from 12:00 PM to 10:00 PM on Sundays.
  2. Do you offer any vegan or gluten-free options? Yes, we have a selection of vegan and gluten-free pizzas available. Please check our menu or ask our staff for more details.
  3. Can I book a table in advance? Absolutely! We recommend making a reservation, especially on weekends. You can book a table by calling us or using our online reservation system.
  4. Do you provide takeout or delivery services? We offer both takeout and delivery services. You can place your order over the phone or through our website.
  5. What makes your Neapolitan pizza authentic? Our pizzas are made with traditional ingredients imported from Italy and baked in a stone oven, following the age-old techniques from Naples.
  6. Is there parking available nearby? Yes, there is ample parking available in the vicinity of the restaurant.
  7. Do you cater to large parties or private events? We love hosting special occasions! Please contact us to discuss how we can make your event memorable.
  8. Do you have a children's menu? Yes, we have a menu specially designed for our younger guests, featuring smaller portions and kid-friendly options.
  9. Are gift cards available for purchase? Yes, we offer gift cards that you can purchase in the restaurant or online. They make a perfect gift for any occasion!

These FAQs aim to address common inquiries and enhance the customer experience at Luigi's Pizzeria. If you have more questions, don't hesitate to reach out to us!
